Laura Shapiro grew up in Long Island, New York. At a very young age, she began a career in commercial and print work with Ford Modeling Agency and later joined Funny-Face Modeling Agency. She was featured in several print adds for companies such as Burlington Coat Factory, Macyâ€™s, and Lei Jeans. She was also featured on an episode of Saturday Night Live.

In high school, she fell in love with opera and musical theater after attending the Metropolitan Opera Intensive, NYU Tisch Summer High School Intensive, and the Central City Opera and Colorado Springs Conservatory Intensive. After that, she was hooked. She was selected for High School All State Choir for three years and continued to participate in musicals and plays throughout her entire high school career.

Laura is a double major at the University of Miami studying Theater Arts and Broadcast Journalism. Her current academic standing is a 3.94. This past year, she was able to participate in a master class with Director Victor Libio and Actress/Singer Lea Salonga.
